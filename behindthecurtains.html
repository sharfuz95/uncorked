Aliye Gallagher

Digital Strategy Advisor

Aliye loves emails. So much so, that she created an entire degree all about email communications! Not really. Aliye graduated in 2019 with an interdepartmental (aka DIY) degree in Digital Communications: Theory, Analysis, Practice. She continued on to California, joining 16 others in the first cohort of Experience Lab at UC Berkeley. Aliye is a strong advocate for alternative educational paths, experiential learning (clearly) and making it up as you go along. She founded and runs a photo and video business for the past six years, which has now expanded into a digital strategy, web design and development, and many forms of digital art firm. Aliye has worked in London and Dublin, and is always looking around the corner for the next opportunity (her dad calls it the 'jedi mind trick').
