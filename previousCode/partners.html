Our Guests

Meet our amazing guests from the industry, follow along to learn their stories.

Evan Basta

Sales Engineer at Cisco

Unlike Sharfuz & Sid, Evan didn't fail a class, he failed multiple. He also graduated from U Rochester (they so didn't meet each other at school) where he was at a music school first for some reason before switching to CSC. He started his own venture at school where he fixed people's computers, rode a Harley Davidson and ate too much Chipotle(update:he still does). He didn't want to force himself just to be a software engineer(unlike many) and crafted his career as a Sales Engineer at Cisco. Unlike convention, it took him 6 years to finish college, but unlike many he hacked his way to finding his passion and what he is good at.

Azmayeen Rhythm

Solutions Engineer at Ring Central

Rhythm graduated from the University of Rochester in 2019 with a double major in Computer Science and Economics. He came to the United States 5 years ago for college. During the past 5 years he studied abroad in the United Kingdom and Hong Kong. Having lived in different cities around the world taught him how to adapt in new, unknown and sometimes even unwanted situations. Currently He is living in Denver, CO among the Rocky Mountains and working as an Associate Solutions Engineer at RingCentral, Inc. In his free time, he like to play the guitar and explore the Rocky mountains surrounding Denver.

Lauren Lindsey

Senior at Loyola Marymount University

Lauren is a rising senior at Loyola Marymount University, with a major in Computer Science and a minor in Business Administration. On campus, she serves as President to the LMU chapter the the Society Women Engineers. She spent her whole life growing up in the Silicon Valley of the Bay Area, only to find herself there again as an intern at Google. After two summers interning at Google, she accepted an offer to be a Software Engineering Intern at the Walt Disney company, which ended up being cancelled due to COVID-19. Luckily, she is now spending her summer as a Software Engineering intern at SAP!

Grace Czechowski

Executive Search Consultant @ Adecco Search

Grace is an Executive search consultant and a Community Champion. She used her leadership skills to navigate new industries, new roles, and new goals. Beginning in aerospace and defense manufacturing, she sought out more personally meaningful opportunities as part of a nonprofit team that helped middle and high school students start their own businesses. Ultimately, she found her passion aligned best with human resources where she could blend her industry knowledge with her desire to help others. She has a wealth of experience and industry knowledge of the recruitment world.

Beatriz Gil

Business Analyst @ Deloitte

Beatriz is a University of Rochester ’19 graduate who is currently working in consulting as a Business Analyst at Deloitte. She is an international student from Spain who wanted to make the most out of her college experience in the US. Hell yeah she did! She decorated her college experience with excellence in academia, research, & campus involvement. She was an Economics and Political Science double major with a double minor in Chinese and International Relations , completed an honors thesis, and was heavily involved with leadership roles in Student Government. Most notably, she made an everlasting contribution to the UR community through extended leadership as a Senator, and in her senior year, as the President of the UR Student Government. If you were a student at UR while she was in college, you definitely saw her smiling face on campus and she had a positive impact in your college experience in some way. She is an awesome person, can rock out to Spanish music better than anyone else, and we are all convinced by now that she is the embodiment of Meliora.
