Persistence Wins Over Bad luck

Saturday, July 11th @ 11:00 AM EST/ 2:00 PM EST

After graduating from the University of Massachusetts, Amherst with a BS in Pre-Med/Biology and a minor in Computer Science, Evis started his career in Enterprise Software Sales at Experian Data Quality in Boston and then at Oracle in San Francisco. Evis then transitioned into Management Consulting at IBM where he helped F500 companies develop and implement IT systems strategies. After six years of enterprise software experience, Evis decided to attend The University of Chicago Booth School of Business to pursue an MBA in the Evening MBA program and transitioned into the early-stage startup world at TrueData where was responsible for Product Development. We will be diving deep into Evis' amazing experiences wearing so many different hats in the industry as well as learning how to get into one of the best MBA programs in the world. Make sure to tune in!
